HOW TO USE:
Show top 20 symbols whose relative strength > VNINDEX (with specific date):
python3 relative_strength.py 2025-11-11

Show top 50:
python3 relative-strength.py 2024-11-11 VNINDEX 50

Show top 20 symbols whose relative strength > VN30:
python3 relative_strength.py 2025-11-11 VN30

Check specific symbol whether its relative strength greater or lower VNINDEX (or a specific INDEX):
python3 relative-strength.py MWG 2025-11-11

Show all symbols whose relative strength > VNINDEX
python3 relative_strength.py 2025-11-11
